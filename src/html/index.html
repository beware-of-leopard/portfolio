The Work

Inside the Park

Design the ultimate baseball road trip

App allows users to easily plan a multi-city road trip seeing as many games in as many parks as they want. Results produce an itinerary with games selected, but also a map of the route and localized travel data (hotels, attractions, food, plane tickets, and car rentals) they can use to intelligently plan their trip.

Technologies used: Javascript (ES6), React, React router, ajax, API integration (SeatGeek, Google Maps, and Skyscanner)

Piano Creations

Full redesign of client site

PianoCreations is the home of an extremely talented pianist named Alex-Zsolt. However, his site hadn't been updated in years and it reflected poorly on his brand as a result.

  • Post redesign, the site is no longer a labyrinth. The new IA is people and SEO friendly. The major tasks (booking Alex for concerts and buying his music) are easier to find and accomplish.
  • I eliminated superfluous copy, much of which was driven by what the client wished the user to know, not what the user was actually seeking.

Technologies used: WordPress, CSS, WooCommerce
